ABOUT US

WĪF is a one-stop supply chain solution applicable for all fashion brands. We understand that sourcing fabrics has been a long, expensive and most challenging process. Here at WĪF, we provide a platform where anyone can source fabrics faster, easier and more efficient through both online and offline.

Experienced first hand, the female founders of WĪF envision the platform to contribute for a much stronger & sustainable ecosystem by streamlining the supply chain process and its current industry imbalances.

SAMPLE ROOM & LIBRARY
